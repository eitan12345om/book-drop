How it works

Open this service on your Kobo, Kindle, or Tolino — it will show a unique 4-character key. Enter that key above, pick a file, and press Upload and send. A download link appears on your e-reader within seconds.

Files are stored temporarily on the server and deleted automatically a few minutes after you close the e-reader browser or generate a new key.
