      How it works

      Open this service on your Kobo, Kindle, or Tolino — it will show a 4-character key and a QR code. Scan the QR code with your phone to open this page with the key pre-filled, or type the key manually. Pick one or more files and press Upload and send. Download links appear on your e-reader within seconds.

      Files are stored temporarily on the server and deleted automatically a few minutes after you close the e-reader browser or generate a new key.
